About Us

Get To Know Pindot Paninda

Who We Are

“PindotPaninda is your go-to online street food stop that brings the vibrant flavors of Filipino kanto favorites like kwek-kwek, fishballs, and kikiam right to your fingertips.

ith love for local food culture, we believe every 'pindot' (click) should lead to crispy, tasty joy!”

Mission

“To deliver classic Filipino street food online with ease, speed, and flavor – making every snack time feel like you’re at your favorite food stall.”

Vision

“To become the top online hub for authentic Filipino street food, bringing people together through shared cravings and delicious bites.”

Our Story

“We started as a simple food cart in 2020 and expanded online during the pandemic, using the internet to bring your tusok-tusok cravings straight to your home. Now, we serve hundreds of happy customers who want that "kanto experience" with just one pindot!”
